* Experience (builtin, vanilla minecraft xp)
* Vault
* CoinsEngine
* PlayerPoints
* RoyaleEconomy
* SuperMobCoins
* UltraEconomy
* KingdomsX
* RivalHarvesterHoes
* TheOnly-MobCoins
* RedisEconomy
* BeastTokens
* EcoBits

## Shulker Viewers
