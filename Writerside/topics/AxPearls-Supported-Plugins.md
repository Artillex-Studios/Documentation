# Supported Plugins

* Team plugin are used for the settings that allows players to throw ender pearls through their own teammates.

### Team Plugins
- AxParties
- BetterTeams
- FactionsUUID
- GangsPlus
- Guilds
- KingdomsX
- Parties
- SaberFactions
- SimpleClans
- SuperiorSkyBlock2
- Towny
- UltimateClans
