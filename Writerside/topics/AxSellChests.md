# AxSellChests 🛠️

> This plugin has not released yet, some things are subject to change!
{style="warning"}

### What is AxSellChests?
- A fast & efficient sell chest and chunk collector plugin.

## Most Important Features
- Auto Sell: The chests sell items in them every few seconds.

![image_173.png](image_173.png)
- Chunk Collect: Collects all items in the chunk and adds them to the chest.

![image_174.png](image_174.png)
- Stacker Plugins Supported: The sell chest can sell all items from the ground without the chest overflowing.
- Charge System: If enabled, sell chests will have a timer which when it expires, the selling stops and has to be recharged.

![image_175.png](image_175.png)
- Bank Integrations: Optionally make sell chest earnings go to the player's (or teams) bank directly.

![image_176.png](image_176.png)
- Boosters: The plugin comes with 2 configurable booster types. (money and speed booster)

![image_177.png](image_177.png)

![image_178.png](image_178.png)

![image_188.png](image_188.png)
- Sell Summary: Display the number of items sold and money made to players every few minutes.

![image_185.png](image_185.png)
- Instant Sell Mode: Optionally sell items instantly when they get dropped in a chunk where sell chests are placed.
- Chest Sell Multiplier: Create chests with any amount of sell multipliers and sell speeds.

![image_186.png](image_186.png)

![image_187.png](image_187.png)
- Whitelist System: You can add your friends to sell chests even if they can't place blocks nearby.

![image_182.png](image_182.png)

![image_181.png](image_181.png)
- Advanced Logging: All interactions with sell chests are tracked, and they can be viewed inside a gui ingame.

![image_183.png](image_183.png)
- Holograms: Display the next sell time, sell statistics, owner name and other information. (can be changed or disabled)

![image_179.png](image_179.png)

![image_180.png](image_180.png)
- Limits: You can set sell chest place limits with a permission or with a command.
